Redesign of my school Homepage

The idea of this website came to me when two friends started a firewood business and sold their products exclusively via eBay. So I created a concept for a page with all the necessary information, including what services they offered and, most important, the contact page.
I also designed a logo for the firewood business and developed a colour scheme to be used throughout the website.

At the time, I was very inspired by a map design, so I always divided each section into a text side and an image side.
